## Formula
**Volume = Length × Width × Thickness**  
Convert thickness to meters or feet consistently, then to **m³** or **yd³**.

### Example
- Length = 10 ft  
- Width = 10 ft  
- Thickness = 4 in = 0.333 ft  
**Volume = 10 × 10 × 0.333 ≈ 33.3 ft³ = 1.23 yd³**

> Tip: Add 5–10% for waste (spillage, uneven grade).
